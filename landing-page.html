IsshoTabe

  

Let's spend time talking and eating together


IsshoTabe is a communication website for university students to socialize while enjoying meals together online. Grab some food, join a video call, and have fun together.




VIDEO CALL

Enjoy communicating with university friends at home


It’s quarantine, and you can’t hang out with friends at the cafeteria as you did before? Create a video meeting and call your friends for a fun time eating together after online lectures!




SCHEDULE

Set a date for eating and hanging out with friends


Invite friends to an online eating gathering beforehand and keep track of it in your schedule!



ADDITIONAL FUNCTIONS

From "Itadakimasu!" to "Kanpai!"


Interesting "eating" functions in the video call for your enjoyable meal together, from lunch to nomikai. Moreover, we also detect munching noises and can help reduce them to your likings.
